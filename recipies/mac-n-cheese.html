Mac 'n Cheese

Back

Overview:A creamy, cheesy comfort dish made with tender pasta coated in a rich cheese sauce.

  1. Boil salted water and cook macaroni until al dente, then drain.
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Whisk flour into butter and cook 1 minute to form a roux.
  4. Slowly whisk in milk, stirring until smooth.
  5. Cook until sauce thickens slightly.
  6. Add shredded cheese and stir until melted.
  7. Season with salt, pepper, and optional mustard.
  8. Combine cooked pasta with cheese sauce.
  9. Transfer to a baking dish if baking is desired.
  10. Bake until bubbly or serve immediately.
  11. ENJOY!
