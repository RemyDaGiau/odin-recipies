Back
  1. Take out box of perogies
  2. Lay out cast iron pan on burner and set heat to medium
  3. Add a thin film of olive oil to the pan
  4. Pour perogies into the pan
  5. While the perogies heat up, begin seasoning:
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Onion Powder
    • Garlic Powder
  6. After cooking for 8 minutes, flip the perogies
  7. Repeat step 5
  8. Wait an additional 8 minutes
  9. Serve and enjoy!
