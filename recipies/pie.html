Overview: A classic dessert with spiced apples baked in a flaky, golden crust.

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Peel, core, and slice apples evenly.
  3. Toss apples with sugar, cinnamon, flour, and lemon juice.
  4. Roll out bottom pie crust and place in pie dish.
  5. Fill crust with apple mixture.
  6. Dot filling with small pieces of butter.
  7. Cover with top crust and seal edges.
  8. Cut slits in the top crust for steam.
  9. Bake until crust is golden and filling bubbles.
  10. Cool before slicing and serving.
  11. ENJOY!
